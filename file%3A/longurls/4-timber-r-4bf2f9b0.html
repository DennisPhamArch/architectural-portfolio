TypePre-studio Research
RangeTimber + Urban Tree Material
FocusReclaimed Material + Product Flow
StatusAcademic project - Studio Research

Overview

This pre-studio research established a broad vision for potential timber sources and material workflow within fabrication facility. In response to the studio objectives and the decision to focus on timber, the research investigates alternatives sources, including timber recovered from demolished and deconstructed buildings, as well as trees removed or trimmed from urban areas. Rather than being discarded or used as firewood, these materials can be reclaimed as valuable and environmentally responsible timber resources.
