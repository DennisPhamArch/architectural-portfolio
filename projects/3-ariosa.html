Overview

Ariosa, a serviced apartment, is situated in the highest construction and traffic concentrationn in Ho Chi Minh city, Vietnam. As a common elongated tube house with 5m x 42m, the issues include a severe lack of light and natural ventilation. The facade is positioned backward to create porch that suits people living in tropical climate. Beside that, wooden frame balconies and plants are caculated to diminish the amount of dust, smoke, and noise from busy road. Courtyards are integrated to cope with the poor ventilation and lack of natural light. All rooms are designed as a small home that are well ventilated and softly sun-kissed under tree canopies.
